Where To Buy The adidas Yeezy Boost 350 v2 “Dazzling Blue”

February 23, 2022 by Jovani Hernandez
 
4.33 / 5 49 VOTES
This post contains references to products from one or more of our advertisers. We may receive compensation when you click on links to those products. The opinions and information provided on this site are original editorial content of Sneaker News.

ADVERTISEMENT

First teased in December 2021, the adidas Yeezy Boost 350 v2 “Dazzling Blue” finally releases on February 26th in adult and kids sizes.

Akin to the “Black Friday” trio that dropped in 2016, the upcoming Yeezys indulge in a pitch “Black” ensemble across its upper and sole unit. Primeknit construction up top, however, is interrupted by a “Dazzling Blue” stripe running the length of the lateral profile; “SPLY-350” text on the detail harkens back to previous Yeezy 350 v2 launches, too. Down below, the ribbed, semi-translucent midsole reveals the tried-and-proven Boost-cushioning that’s appears on handfuls of Yeezy silhouettes over the last seven years.

Enjoy another set of images of the pair ahead (courtesy of Instagram user @sneakertigger), and find the shoes arriving to select Yeezy stockists on Saturday, February 26th.

Elsewhere under the Three Stripes umbrella, the adidas NMD S1 is gradually putting together its roster.
